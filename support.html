Support

Troubleshooting, contact, and launch support.

Use this page as the support destination linked from GitHub, Netlify, or your final store listing.

Quick fixes

  • Confirm notification access is enabled for Notisaver.
  • Disable battery optimization for reliable background capture.
  • Verify the source app is not blocked inside per-app controls.
  • Restart the listener service after major Android updates.

What to include in support email

  • Android version and device model
  • App version or preview build details
  • Short description of the problem
  • Whether the issue affects capture, export, backup, or lock features

Common questions

Check notification access, power management restrictions, and whether the source app is excluded in Notisaver settings.

The amount of content available depends on what the original app exposes through Android notifications.

Store exports only in trusted locations, use device-level protection, and prefer encrypted backup flows where available.
